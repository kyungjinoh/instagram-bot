⚙️ HOW IT WORKS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Opens Instagram account → Clicks Followers → Scrolls & collects ALL 
usernames → Visits each profile → Checks bios → Follows if keyword 
matches → Respects limits → Moves to next account

⏱️ RATE LIMITS (AUTOMATIC)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Max 5 follows per hour (enforced automatically)
• Waits 1 hour after 5th follow
• Resumes automatically after wait
• No total follow limit (continues until all accounts done)
